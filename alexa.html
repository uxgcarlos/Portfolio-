Alexa, Open my window

Alexa, Open my window

Hackathon Sun Code - Powerhouse
My Team Ravi Bhandi (Data Analyst), Ryan Kladar (Software Engineer)
My Role Designed the interface of an app that would open and close windows that would connect to a raspberry pi or Amazon Alex
Duration 7-8 Hours
Methods Comparative analysis, Interviewing, Archetypes
Tools Pen and Paper, Figma

Overview

Our users would be able to open their windows through our custom app that would be able to connect to countless smart home devices such as nest, and security systems. Coming into the Hackathon one of my teammates already had a hardware prototype of the actual device that would connect to the window. The device would be using raspberry pi to actually take orders from the user. Due to that I focused on designing an app and building an interactive prototype while my other two teammates focused on building the algorithm for the product.

The Problem

Windows are a massively overlooked part of energy efficiency and home comfort.

The Solution

Building a product that opens windows electrically which will not only solve our users current problem but will also save energy during the process.
Try out our interactive prototype

Key Takeaways

  • How to do things quickly with purpose
  • Time management - That setting a set schedule for the different parts of the design was crucial in making the deadline
  • How to efficiently collaborate with a scrum team to understand what data they are collecting so I can design it to let users understand the data

"I hate when I forget to open my windows before I leave the house"

This was my teammates product so I sat down with him and interviewed him to get to know more about the problem and how this product could better assist him.

Key Insights

  • He was having to manually crank open his windows which is a hassle
  • If he forgot to open his windows before he left to work when he would come home his room would be unbearably hot and stuffy
From conducting the interview I was able to create an archetype that would be the basis of our design.

Archetype

Lives in a house with multiple hand crank windows that he has to open and close several times a day. If he doesnâ€™t open his windows in the morning when he gets home his house is gets really stuffy and unbearably hot.

Needs

  • Needs an easy way to open windows
  • Needs to know when and what time of day is best to open his windows

Pain Points

  • Time consuming to crank several windows
  • House temperature is really hot

Getting My Ideas Out

I wanted to first sketch out my design because it helps me get out all of my ideas. I utilized this to also run over the design my team before I actually start the higher fidelity wireframes.

  • I utilized the toggle feature since the user is going to be able to see an immediate reaction when toggling their widows open or closed.
  • I gave the users an option to customize how open they wanted their window. For this I designed a slider that would have three pre determined options (Closed, Half way open, and Fully open). This feature gives the users flexibility on how open they want their windows while also not being too overwhelming.
  • I explored an idea that would give the user tips and ways to put their product to best use . This would have tips on when to open their windows to achieve maximum energy conservation while also cooling their house.

Hi-Fi Wire Frames

Homepage Key Insights

  • The first tile is to inform the user on how much they are saving from using this app. This is going to give the users some reassurance on why they should keep using the app / product.
  • The second tile is the house temperature where they would beable to see their current (just in case they would want to change it and set a new temp) , how many windows are open( for security reasons we would let the users know how many windows they have open), and their current indoor air quality (the users would be able to open their windows to improve their indoor air quality) .
  • The weather tile is to give the user more resources in determining whether they should close or open their windows .
  • Kept the Navigation to three easy options which is the Homepage, Toggle feature to open their windows, and an account page which wold allow them to connect to other smart home devices.

Window Control Key Insights

  • The invite home member feature gives the user a easy way to give access to other home members.
  • The toggle feature gives the user an easy way to open / close their windows.
  • The affordances affordances on the page provide the user with supporting information that let them know if their window is open or closed.
